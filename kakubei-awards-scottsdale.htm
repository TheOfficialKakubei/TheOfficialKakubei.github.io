Master Japanese and Chinese Calligraphy Awards and Honors by Kakubei



 

The following are Kakubei’s Awards and Honors received from the Hesei Calligraphy Federation 平成書道連盟. Each month calligraphers submit pre-designated pieces to the federation for judging. Prizes are awarded monthly and announced in the federation’s publication 平成書道研究. Prizes and Honors are awarded for individuals at the Masters levels and at the non-Masters levels.
